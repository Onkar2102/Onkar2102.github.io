Supervised Learning

Business Insights on Post-COVID Taxi Systems

Tens of thousands of commuters in NYC rely on taxis as a mode of transportation around the bustling metropolis. Data was stored on the cloud and its processing was done for Analytics. Using Machine Learning practices, the algorithm was created for predicting the fares pricing, and deployment of the model was achieved with Flask. Business insights from the processed data were extracted and the possible effect of COVID on future business was analyzed. Lastly, reporting was done using PowerBI.
